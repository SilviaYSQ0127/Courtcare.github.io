17 March 2004 The defendant appeared at the Local Court of New South Wales in answer to the charges.

The defendant destroyed or damaged the victim's property, and their mental condition caused psychological fear of harm to the victim.

The defendant may have a mental health impairment or cognitive impairment, but it is not a mentally ill or disordered person within the meaning of Chapter 3 of the Mental Health Act 2007.

Dismissed. The defendant is discharged into the care of a responsible person on the condition that a treatment plan attached is recorded.
